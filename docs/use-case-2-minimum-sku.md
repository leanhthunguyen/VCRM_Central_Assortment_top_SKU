## Observations

- **Highest non-compliance:** TB_IQ (Iraq) at 48.9% — nearly half of vendors are below the minimum SKU threshold
- **Emerging markets** (GV_NG, GV_TN, GV_KG) show 40%+ non-compliance — expected for newer markets with less vendor maturity
- **Mature markets** like PY_AR (Argentina) at 4.7% show strong assortment compliance
- **Large opportunity in Turkey:** YS_TR has 8,794 vendors below threshold — the highest absolute count
